GLOBAL CHALLENGE PROGRAMS

A program in which Linton School students explore countries or related industries they are interested in and experience industrial sites.

Through voluntary planning and investigation, the company submits a plan to experience the industry, business or culture of the Emerging Markets to the business group, and supports the relevant expenses with scholarships after the review of the project group.
